## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** analyzing **5 years (FY 2021–2025)** of sales, profitability, customer, and operational data for **Four Clothing .CO** — a multi-channel fashion retail brand operating across **Nigeria and international markets**.

The dashboard is designed across **6 interactive pages**:

| Page | Focus Area |
|------|-----------|
| 🏠 **Executive Summary** | High-level KPIs, revenue & profit trends, YTD comparisons |
| 👗 **Product Performance** | Collection revenue, profitability, materials, pricing & returns |
| 👥 **Customer Insights** | Segmentation, purchase channels, ratings & retention |
| 📍 **Location Analysis** | Regional revenue, orders, profit & shipping costs |
| 🚀 **Growth & Scalability** | Social reach, delivery performance & revenue forecast |
| 🎯 **Strategic Summary** | Data-driven recommendations for business growth |

## 🎯 Strategic Recommendations

![Strategic Summary Dashboard](<Dashboard Learning Page.png>)

Based on the data analysis, the following strategic actions are recommended:

1. **📦 Expand High-Performing Collections** — Scale Corporate Wear, Footwear, and Streetwear with new SKUs and seasonal drops to capitalize on proven demand.

2. **🌐 Double Down on B2C Channels** — Strengthen Website D2C and WhatsApp commerce to reverse the 2025 revenue softness and improve margins by reducing intermediary costs.

3. **🔄 Boost Customer Retention** — The 54.6% repeat rate is solid but has room to grow. Implement loyalty programs, personalized email campaigns, and post-purchase engagement strategies.

4. **👑 Introduce Premium Pricing** — Build a premium pricing model for VIP customers with exclusive collections, early access, and personalized experiences to maximize revenue per customer.

5. **🌍 Scale International Markets** — The international segment currently at $1.00M has significant untapped potential. Explore partnerships, targeted marketing, and localized offerings for growth.
